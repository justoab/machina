ARTISTS

Lorenzo

After an extense music journey through various eastern european countries, Lorenzo Sinaloa has managed to come out with some fine organic sounds mixed up with modular synth stabs and breaks giving him a unique sound which can easily be identified in any club or festival.

Angela

Born and raised in Damasco, Syria. She's been releasing house music since an adolescent, shaping her sound since then. It is reasonable to say Angela's drum grooves are one of the most fun and original in the business right now due to her particular sound picking and drum-sampling.

Augusto

With a 30-years-long music career, Augusto Contreras could be hands down considered one of the pioneers of electronic music. Based in south Detroit he lived half of his life surrounded by house records and some well-known names in the music industry such as Juan Atkins, Derrick May and some others.

VISIONS AND IDEAS

does capitalism hold the door to fame open to anyone who wants it for a few minutes? This decisive moment—what does it take?

Staying power. Resisting temptations. It is a unique art; you can’t push too hard for something to happen. Having the self-assurance to let it take its course.
